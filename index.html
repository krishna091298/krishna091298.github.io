Welcome to Pandurang Agro Industries

Empowering Quality and Sustainability.

Pandurang Agro Industries in Latur is a leading processor and supplier of high-quality pulses (dall) in the region since 2009. With state-of-the-art machinery and a focus on quality, the company is committed to delivering premium products that meet the diverse culinary needs of its customers. Every batch of dall processed in the unit is guaranteed to meet the highest standards. Both partners of Pandurang Agro Industries are women entrepreneurs, and the increasing presence of women in entrepreneurship has led to significant business and economic growth. The partners of the firm are experienced industrialists hailing from a family with extensive knowledge of agriculture. They can be considered Agri-entrepreneurs, committed to developing the agri-based industry.
